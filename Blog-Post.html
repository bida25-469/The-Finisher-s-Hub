What I Learnt From Lionel Messi🐐

Lionel Messi is more than just a football player, he is a lesson in life. Watching him play shows how great and smart he is. He reads the game before any player would.He positions himself well and knows where the ball goes on the field. I learnt that when you got composure , you got everything.Messi never panicks when he has the ball.He takes his time , find a spot to score and finishes.He inspires me a lot that i try to apply what he does everytime i have a game.I always remind myself to always stay calm and just do the work.

How To Improve Decision-making And Dribbling

As a striker, decision-making and dribbling is important.As a striker i need to know when to shoot, when to pass and when to hold the ball.I watch match footages to improve and ask myself what i would do in each situation. Dribbling is all about ball control and work of pace. Practising drills every day sharpens my first touch and the abilities to dribble the defenders and score goals. I learnt to always dribble with purpose because if not you wwill not have a plan and the ball may be taken from you.

My Life As A Striker

Being a striker is one of the positions with pressure in football, most of the people expects a goal. Playing for Mexican Girls has really taught me patience,ability to focus and have mental strength.There are times where I miss chances of scoring and nothing goes right, and I take that as a test character. I learnt that everytime I miss a goal I should not focus more on that but shake it off and wait for the next opportunities to score . A striker with a short memory for mistakes and long memory for scoring is a smart player.

My Journey As A Striker

My journey as a striker was not easy as I thought. I had to work hard to earn my position and be the best I could. I had to focus on finishes, positioning and movement of the ball.As I started learning these I got understand the game better and began to have confidence in myself.Lionel Messi is my role model because he showed me that even the smallest player can perfom well with the right mindset.My aim is to score as many goals as I can each game , keep composure and make better decision making.
