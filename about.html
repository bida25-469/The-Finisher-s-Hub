Atin:The Finisher's Mind⚽

About Atin

My name is Amogelang Atin.On the field I call myself Atin, a striker playing for Mexican Girls Social Club.I am from Molapowabojang but I currently stay in Gaborone.

My main goal is to score as many goals as I can each game and keep composure and not panic under pressure.

My Football Story

I started playing football in grade 5 and while playing on the streets, I realized how passionate I was about the sport.A girl as young as I was kicking a ball has grown into a dedicated striker.

My Role Model

Lionel Messi is the reason why I became a striker because watching him play showed me that it is not about power but composure, intelligence and reading the game.He inspired me to believe that it is not about how big or small you are, if you are passionate and believe in yourself, you can perform well on the pitch.Everytime I play as a striker I try to bring the Lionel mentality to the game.

My Goals

My biggest dream is to represent Botswana at the highest level and play for the ladies national team.I want to up my game so I improve my skills while playing for Mexican Girls and play professionally outside the country.Every training sessions, every goals I score drives me closer to what I want to achieve.

My Everyday Quote

"Every practice, every goal, every game is one step closer to glory."
